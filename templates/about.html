About Music Score Cropper

Music Score Cropper is a web application designed to help musicians optimize their PDF music scores for viewing on tablets, especially iPads. By automatically cropping unnecessary margins from your sheet music, you can enjoy larger, more readable notes and annotations.

Why Crop PDF Scores?

Many PDF music scores contain large margins that waste valuable screen space. When viewing these scores on tablets or e-readers, the actual music notation becomes smaller than it needs to be. By cropping these margins, you can:

  • See larger musical notation
  • Reduce eye strain during practice
  • Make better use of your tablet's screen
  • Improve readability, especially for complex scores

Features

  • Simple drag-and-drop interface
  • Adjustable margin cropping (5% to 25%)
  • Fast processing
  • Secure - files are automatically deleted after processing
  • No registration required

Privacy

Your uploaded files are processed on the server and immediately deleted after the cropped version is generated. We do not store or share your music scores.
